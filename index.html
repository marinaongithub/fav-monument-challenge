Dunnottar Castle

Discover the Majesty of Dunottar Castle: Where History Meets Breathtaking Beauty!

Dunottar Castle is a historic fortress located on the northeastern coast of Scotland, overlooking the North Sea. It is a stunning medieval fortress with a rich history that dates back to the Early Middle Ages.

Explore the breathtaking views and learn about the castle's fascinating history as you walk through its ruins and grounds.
